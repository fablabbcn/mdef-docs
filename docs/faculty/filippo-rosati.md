Founder, Creative Director of Umanesimo Artificiale and Operating System Studio
Since founding Umanesimo Artificiale in 2017, he has been working across art, science, and exponential technologies. His approach is rooted in antidisciplinarity, exploring the connections between art, design, robotics, biology, and hacking. He maintains a deeply experimental practice, merging artistic and scientific research to foster new forms of creative inquiry and innovation.
Umanesimo Artificiale works has been exhibited at Ars Electronica, Sónar Festival, RomaEuropa Festival, Videocittà, RoBot Festival, WeSa Seoul, Benetton Foundation, Embassy of France in Rome (Italy), among others.
